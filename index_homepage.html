Welcome to the Stockholm Music Festival 2025!

In June 2025 Stockholm’s stages will welcome amazing musicians to celebrate the beginning of summer with great music. Whether you prefer jazz, pop, metal, R&B, indie, or hip-hop, there’s a concert you’ll love. Let’s kick off the upcoming summer in the best possible way and immerse ourselves in the unforgettable atmosphere of the festival together!
